Master's Program at SVSU

‌

Contemporary business practices are data driven, supported by computer information systems of ever more extreme complexity. Contemporary engineering and manufacturing practices also require vast amounts of data supplied by computer systems that integrate with powerful and diverse electronic hardware. SVSU's Masters of Science in Computer Science and Information Systems program will help you soar, whether your goal is to automate manufacturing processes, secure an Internet-of-Things implementation, optimize information systems enterprises, or build the next killer app.

Choose Class Options Convenient For You

Available for Fall 2017, the MS-CSIS program will offer courses through three delivery methods:

1) Face-to-face, classes on the University Center campus; offered in the evenings, starting at 7 p.m.;
2) Hybrid, a blend of face-to-face and online; offered in the evenings, starting at 7 p.m.;
3) Online, to take at a time convenient for you!

Why SVSU

Our Master of Science in Computer Science and Information Systems (MS-CSIS) sets itself apart by incorporating advanced topics in an applied, hands-on educational environment, including traditional computer science topics as well as a special emphasis on information systems. With this approach, students benefit from more business and practical applications, complementing SVSU’s vision of experiential learning.
